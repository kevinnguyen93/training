In order to add some behavior to our application, we need a controller. Add a controller named StoreController to our gemStore application.

Objectives
1.3-1 Add a controller named StoreController to our gemStore application.
1.3-2 Attach the StoreController to the <body> tag. Be sure to alias it as store.
1.3-3 In app.js, we've added a gem object to represent one of the products in our gemStore. 
1.3-3 Assign it to the product property of StoreController so we can use them in the page.
1.3-4 Display the name of the product inside the <h3> tag.
1.3-5 Display the price of the product inside the <em> tag.
